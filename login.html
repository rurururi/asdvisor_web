Parent Consent

We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your information on our mobile app. As part of our efforts to provide comprehensive healthcare services, we request your consent for the secure storage and encrypted access of both parent and child information by authorized healthcare professionals.

By clicking the "I Agree" button below, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

  • Secure Storage: We employ robust encryption measures to safeguard the confidentiality and integrity of the information provided by parents and associated with their child on our mobile app.
  • Authorized Access: Only authorized healthcare professionals, directly involved in the care of your child, will have access to the stored information. This access is strictly limited to ensure the highest standards of confidentiality.
  • Doctor-Parent Comunication: The encrypted information may be shared between doctors and parents for the purpose of facilitating effective communication related to the health and well-being of your child.
  • Complaince with Regulations: We adhere to all relevant data protection regulations and guidelines to ensure that your information is handled with the utmost care and in compliance with applicable laws.
